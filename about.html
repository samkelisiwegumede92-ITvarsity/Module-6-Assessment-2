ABOUT US

Encompassing over about 130 stores and eateries, Watercrest Mall is the ultimate regional shopping destinaiton. Watercrest Mall embraces the upper highway ethos, being a friendly, warm and welcoming neighbor. Delighting in celebrating the beauty and bouty of this special corner of South Africa, the 44,000sqm mall is located in the suburb of Waterfall in Durban, KwaZulu Natal. Durban city centre is only a 30 minute drive from Watercrest, and there is easy acces to both the N3 and M13. The mall is enchored by Woolworths, Superspar, Checkers, Game, and Dis-chem. Specials features include a much needed Ster Kinekor movie theatre and family oriented food court. Watercrest Mall is managed by Growthpoint.
